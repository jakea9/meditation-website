Technique

Read the instructions before playing the music

  1. Play the music.
  2. Stand still and focus your attention on the music.
  3. When your mind starts to think, bring your attention back to the repetitive beats of the music.
  4. After a minute start to dance slowly and include in your awareness the physical sensations of your movements.
  5. Bring your attention back to the present moment when your mind drifts off.
  6. Speed up the dancing to your taste.
